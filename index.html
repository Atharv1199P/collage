Welcome to Sanjay Ghodawat Institute

Sanjay Ghodawat Institute of Technology (SGIT) offers various IT and Software courses, including B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) and Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA). The university emphasizes developing creative IT professionals with strong technical skills and industry-relevant knowledge. SGIT provides a well-equipped infrastructure with modern labs, experienced faculty, and a curriculum designed to meet industry demands.
