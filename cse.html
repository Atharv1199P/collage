Departments of Computer Science and Engineering(CSE)

About Our Department

  1. The Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) department focuses on the study of computers and computing, encompassing both theoretical foundations and practical applications. It covers areas like computer architecture, programming languages, software development, and computational science, with specializations in fields like AI, data science, and cybersecurity. CSE graduates find diverse career opportunities in software development, data science, AI, cybersecurity, and more.
  2. Established to provide quality education in computer science and prepare students for the IT industry and higher studies. Focus on practical learning through labs, projects, coding assignments, and real-world problem solving.
  3. Subjects include Data Structures, Algorithms, Operating Systems, DBMS, Networks, AI, ML, IoT, and Cybersecurity. Well-equipped computer labs with high-speed internet and latest development tools and software.
  4. Experienced faculty members with academic and industry backgrounds, supporting research and innovation. Strong industry connections for internships, certifications, and placement support.
  5. Regular workshops, seminars, and hackathons to boost technical skills and creativity.Active student clubs and technical forums for peer learning and collaboration.
  6. Consistently good placement record with top recruiters like Infosys, TCS, Capgemini, and Wipro.
  7. Students work on real-world and industry projects. Encouraged to publish papers and develop prototypes. Support for innovation and entrepreneurship.
